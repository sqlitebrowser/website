Thanks to our Patreon supporters, we have a HiDPI monitor!

2021-03-06

A special thanks also goes to Nikolay Zlatev of Astra Paging too, for organising the purchase and shipping of the monitor around the EU.

This should enable - or at least strongly help - with fixing the remaining HiDPI related bugs that people have reported over the years.
